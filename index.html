A man who found a Vaccine

Here's a time line of Dr. Manuel's life:

  • 1946 - Born in Colombia
  • 1987 - The vaccine candidate, first developed in 1987 in Colombia, was evaluated in clinical trials carried out by the WHO in Gambia, Tanzania and Thailand, and had mixed results.
